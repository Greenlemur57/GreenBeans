Simple Green Bean Recipe

  1. Wash and trim the ends of 1 pound of fresh green beans.
  2. Boil in salted water for 4-5 minutes until crisp-tender.
  3. Drain and toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Optional: Add a squeeze of lemon or toasted almonds!
